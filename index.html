About me

I'm Francisco Pacio... That doesn't say much, huh?
Well, I graduated from high school as a computer technician in 2020. Now I study Computer Science at Universidad de Buenos Aires.

I introduced myself into programming back in high school. Learned some QBasic64 (yes, QBasic64...) VBasic .NET. The projects we made didn't do much, many were only calculators or typical CRUD apps. Later on, we started using Visual FoxPro 9.0 and included relational databases with SQL Server.

The interesting part begins now. We used MVC-Model to make Bomberman on Python and an Ecommerce on C#. Then we learned about concurrent programming to make a COVID-19 simulator, image filters, and an online chat with sockets on C++.

By the time I write this, I'm preparing myself for a GCP Associate Cloud Engineer Exam. You can check my badges at my GCP Qwiklabs Profile. I'm also developing a program that renders an image based on lighting and objects in C for Algorithms Class.

Special thanks to all my teachers for making me love what they taught me. Fede, Fran, Mastro and Malva! <3
And also thanks to the internet community that gave everyone the gift of information.
